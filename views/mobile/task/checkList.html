Checking of tyre.

Checking of vehicle body.
  a) All doors are able to open and close properly
  b) Battery terminal, terminal insulators mounting bracket are intact and secured
  c) No damage to electrical connector
  d) All mirrors are intact; NOT loose and NOT blurred
  e) Windscreen not shattered or crack
  f) All Seat Belts are able to buckle & lock properly
  g) Seat are secured
Checking of vehicle functions.
